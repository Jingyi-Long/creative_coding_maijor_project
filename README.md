## Inspiration

Our project is inspired by __Composition VIII (1923)__ by Wassily Kandinsky, a geometric abstract painting that explores the relationship between colour, shape, rhythm, and emotion. Kandinsky believed that visual art could function similarly to music, where forms and colours create emotional movement and harmony. We were particularly inspired by the balance between structured geometry and energetic composition within the artwork.

Our reinterpretation transforms the static painting into an interactive digital experience using motion, sound, and user interaction. We were also inspired by Google Arts & Culture’s “Play a Kandinsky”, which explores how Kandinsky’s artworks can be experienced through sound and interaction. Additional inspiration comes from generative art and motion graphics that combine abstract geometry, audiovisual response, and organic movement to create immersive digital environments.
